Welcome to City of Wisdom, a Family of Faith dedicated to the teachings of the Bible and uplifting worship. We extend a warm invitation to individuals of all ages - children, youth, and adults – to be ministered to and discover meaningful opportunities to serve the Lord by serving others. At City of Wisdom, we pride ourselves on being a welcoming and inclusive community where everyone is valued. Our mission is clear: "To Inform, transform and reform; To Follow the Leadership of the Holy Spirit; and to Take God's Word to God's World." Come join us and experience the power of worship, prayer and the word of God.
