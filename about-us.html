City of Wisdom is a thriving church located in Accra, Alhaji-Israel. Established in 2014, formerly know as Royal Believers Ministry(RBM) founded by Reverend Daniel Yaw Donkoh popularly know as Counsellor D Y Donkoh. The church has been serving the local community for about ten years, providing a welcoming and inclusive space for individual to come together and worship.

Throughout our history, City of Wisdom has played an integral role in the community and nation at large, actively participating in various outreach programs, marriage and relationship seminars. From organizing food drives to supporting local charities, the church consistently seeks opportunities to make a positive difference and address the needs of those around them.

Aligned with its mission to spread love and share the message of faith, City of Wisdom offers a range of ministries, including Transfiguratiion, Night of Glory, Eben-ezah (Worship Night), Matters of the Heart, Evangelism, Youth Week Celebartion, Womens Week Celebration, Charity Donation. These initiatives provide opportunities for individuals to enage in meaningful worship, connect with others and contribute to the betterment of society.
